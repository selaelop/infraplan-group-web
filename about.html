Welcome to Infraplan Group

INFRAPLAN GROUP is an established, 100% black-owned civil engineering company specializing in OHS, Environmental management and civil engineering consulting services through INFRAPLAN ENGINEERS.

We also offer civil engineering construction, renovations and general building through INFRAVISION PROJECT, a subsidiary of infraplan group.

At INFRAPLAN GROUP we believe that early identification and effective management of risks are fundamental skills required for sustainability and that's what sets us apart from our competitors.

Our Objective

To create, improve and protect the environment in which we live by planning, designing systems and overseeing construction and maintenance of our infrastructure to ensure a sustainable co-existence of the natural environment and the built environment. We aim to become a catalyst in promoting access to clean water, safe roads, schools, healthcare facilities and other social infrastructure through innovation, competitiveness and transformation of the civil engineering space.

Our Values

People, Quality, Ethics, Integrity, Transformation and Innovation
