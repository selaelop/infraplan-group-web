Civil Construction & General Building

Through our subsidiary Infravision Project, Infraplan Group delivers high-quality civil construction and general building services. We are a CIDB-registered contractor with proven capability to undertake complex civil works and building construction projects for both public and private sector clients across South Africa.

Our construction teams combine technical expertise, modern plant and equipment, and a commitment to quality, safety, and on-time delivery to bring engineering designs to life. We self-perform the majority of our construction work, giving clients confidence in our accountability and quality of workmanship.

Road Construction & Upgrade

We construct new roads and upgrade existing roads from gravel to paved standard, including earthworks, pavement layers, drainage structures, kerbs, road markings, and signage.

Water & Sewer Reticulation

Our construction teams install water supply and sewerage pipeline systems including all associated structures, manholes, connections, testing, and commissioning to hand over fully functional systems.

Residential & Commercial Building

Infravision Project constructs residential housing units, commercial buildings, and community facilities including schools, clinics, and municipal offices to NHBRC and SANS standards.

Renovations & Refurbishment

We undertake renovation and refurbishment projects for existing buildings and infrastructure, restoring functionality, improving performance, and extending the operational life of assets.

Our Construction Capabilities Include

  • Bulk earthworks and site clearing
  • Concrete works (foundations, slabs, retaining walls)
  • Stormwater drainage construction
  • General building maintenance and repairs
  • Social housing construction (RDP/BNG)
  • CIDB-registered for both CE (Civil Engineering) and GB (General Building) categories
