Occupational Health & Safety Consulting

Infraplan Group's OHS Consulting division is dedicated to helping organisations create safe and healthy working environments. We provide expert occupational health and safety consulting services that are fully aligned with South African OHS legislation, including the Occupational Health and Safety Act (Act 85 of 1993) and the Construction Regulations.

We believe that zero harm is achievable through strong safety leadership, effective management systems, and a proactive safety culture. Our team of certified safety professionals partners with clients to identify hazards, assess risks, and implement robust controls that protect workers and stakeholders.

OHS Management Systems

We develop, implement, and audit occupational health and safety management systems tailored to the specific risks and operational requirements of each client, aligned with ISO 45001 and South African legislation.

Safety Audits & Inspections

Our safety professionals conduct comprehensive OHS audits and site inspections to evaluate compliance with legislation, identify gaps, and provide clear recommendations for improvement with practical action plans.

Risk Assessments & Hazard Identification (HIRA)

We facilitate systematic hazard identification and risk assessments (HIRA) for construction, operations, and maintenance activities, producing clear risk registers with practical control measures.

Construction Health & Safety

Infraplan Group provides Construction Health and Safety Agents and OHS consultancy for construction projects, including preparation of Health & Safety Specifications and Health & Safety Plans in compliance with the Construction Regulations.

Our OHS Services Include

  • Legal compliance audits and gap analyses
  • Health and Safety induction and awareness training
  • Incident investigation and reporting
  • Emergency response planning
  • Occupational hygiene assessments
  • COVID-19 workplace readiness plans
