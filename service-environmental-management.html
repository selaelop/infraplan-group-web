Environmental Management Solutions

Infraplan Group is committed to the sustainable development of infrastructure and the protection of the natural environment. Our Environmental Management division provides integrated environmental consulting services that help organisations navigate complex environmental legislation, minimise environmental impacts, and achieve regulatory compliance.

We believe that responsible engineering must respect and protect the ecological systems upon which communities depend. Our environmental professionals bring deep expertise in South African environmental law, ecology, and impact assessment methodologies.

Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA)

We conduct comprehensive Basic Assessments and full EIA processes in compliance with the National Environmental Management Act (NEMA), coordinating all specialist studies and stakeholder engagement required for environmental authorisation.

Environmental Management Programmes (EMPr)

Our team develops detailed Environmental Management Programmes that set out mitigation measures, monitoring protocols, and reporting requirements to ensure construction and operational activities comply with authorisation conditions.

Water Use Licences (WUL)

We prepare Water Use Licence applications for infrastructure projects that affect water resources, including documentation of water use activities and impacts in accordance with the National Water Act.

Environmental Compliance Monitoring

Infraplan Group provides Environmental Control Officer (ECO) services during construction to monitor compliance with authorisation conditions and Environmental Management Programmes, and to advise contractors on best environmental practice.

Additional Environmental Services

  • Strategic Environmental Assessment (SEA)
  • Biodiversity and ecological screening assessments
  • Wetland delineation and impact assessment
  • Waste management planning and licensing
  • Contaminated site investigation and remediation planning
  • Air quality impact assessments
