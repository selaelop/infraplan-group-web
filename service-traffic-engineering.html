Traffic Engineering Services

Infraplan Group provides a full suite of traffic engineering services that support safe, efficient, and well-planned transportation networks. Our traffic engineers work with municipalities, developers, and government agencies to deliver solutions that enhance mobility and minimise congestion.

We apply cutting-edge modelling tools and our deep industry knowledge to design traffic systems that serve communities now and into the future. Our approach integrates safety, sustainability, and economic efficiency in every project we undertake.

Traffic Impact Assessments (TIA)

We conduct thorough traffic impact assessments for developments of all sizes, evaluating the effect on surrounding road networks and proposing effective mitigation measures to ensure smooth traffic flow.

Transport Planning

Our transport planning services help municipalities and developers create integrated transport networks that efficiently connect communities, support economic growth, and reduce travel times.

Road Safety Audits

We carry out formal road safety audits to identify hazards on existing and proposed road designs, recommending practical improvements to reduce accidents and protect all road users.

Traffic Signal Design

Infraplan Group designs and optimises traffic signal systems, including intersection analysis, signal timing plans, and coordination of signals along corridors to improve traffic flow and safety.

Our Expertise Includes

  • Parking studies and design
  • Pedestrian and cyclist infrastructure planning
  • Traffic management plans for construction sites
  • Public transport integration studies
  • Speed management programmes
