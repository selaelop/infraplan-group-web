Water & Sanitation Engineering

Infraplan Group delivers comprehensive Water & Sanitation engineering services to municipalities, local governments, and private clients across South Africa. Our team of experienced civil engineers designs, plans, and oversees the implementation of water infrastructure systems that are sustainable, cost-effective, and compliant with national standards.

We understand that access to clean, safe water and effective sanitation is a fundamental human right and a cornerstone of public health and economic development. Our solutions are tailored to meet the unique needs of each community we serve, from small rural settlements to large urban centres.

Sewerage Reticulation

We design and implement efficient sewer networks that safely collect and convey wastewater from homes and businesses to treatment facilities. Our designs prioritise minimal environmental impact and long-term durability.

Water Supply & Distribution

Our team engineers potable water supply and distribution systems, ensuring reliable access to clean water. We handle everything from source identification and pipeline design to pump stations and pressure management.

Water Storage Solutions

We design reservoirs and water storage facilities that ensure a consistent water supply even during peak demand periods or supply interruptions, including elevated tanks and ground-level reservoirs.

Water Treatment

Infraplan Group provides engineering solutions for water treatment works, ensuring that water meets the standards required by the South African National Standard (SANS 241) before distribution to end users.
